Games

60 Parsecs! Rougelike

60 Parsecs! is game made by polish developer Robot Gentleman. My contribute to this game started 4 months before Steam launch, and nominally supposed to cover implementive work. During this time however i was also working on game with Design and QA teams, solving design problems and finding and sometimes resolving game bugs.

Game page Steam

Mailman Platformer

Mail-Man is game created as submission for Game Maker's Toolkit Gamejam. It took us (Me and Atezai) two, really compressed and fun days to deliver this one. My work here was mostly on 3D art, design and QA.

Game page Itch.io

Bad Call Adventure

The game was made as university project. More about game itself you can find on Atezai page. We were unable to develop more because of lack of time and 'manpower', yet we made a lot of story and world design. I was responisble for 3D art and design in this project.

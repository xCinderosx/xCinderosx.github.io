About me

Overview


I'm a Game Design student, living in Poznań. I'm interested in game dev, graphics especially. My workshop is mostly Blender 3D, Gimp, Canon camera (For texturing) and Unity, but i work also with everything that is necessarily and appropriate, and I'm always open on new, interesting software. Houdini 17 for that instance is one of my next personal target to learn and make use of.


Experience


I am 23 yo, but my first job I got in age of 15. Since then i wasn't unemployeed for more than one month. For all this years, i was working in variety of trades: sale, gardening, paperwork. Now I am employed as Game Designer at Robot Gentleman studio and I'm finishing my engineer degree in Collegium Da Vinci. With such, 8 years experience, I've learned how to cooperate in teams, keep order in work and being self organized well. And a lot of other usefull inwork skills (Coffee brewing included).


Education


As IT student i have to understand logic thinking. For almost four years i was learning programming, and i've managed to learn objective oriented languages as C++/C#/JS. But also Low-level ones as Pure C and Assembler aswell as webdev oriented HTML/CSS/PHP.
